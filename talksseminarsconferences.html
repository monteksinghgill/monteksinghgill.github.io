    Seminars

    Student Homotopy Theory Seminar

    I am the founder and was the organizer of the Student Homotopy Theory Seminar.

    Student Geometry & Topology Seminar

    I was a co-organizer of the Student Geometry & Topology Seminar from Fall 2017 to Winter 2019.

    Homotopy Type Theory Reading Seminar

    In the Winter semester of 2018, I was part of a reading seminar on homotopy type theory. This included, for example, reading Kapulkin-Lumsdaine's ''The Simplicial Model of Univalent Foundations (after Voevodsky)''.

    Machine Learning Reading Seminar

    In the Winter and Fall semesters of 2018, I was part of a reading seminar on machine learning, working through Mohri-Rostamizadeh-Talwalkar's ''Foundations of Machine Learning'' (mostly chapters 1-5), and then further specialized topics.

    Symplectic Reading Group

    In the Winter semester of 2018, I was part of a reading group on symplectic geometry.
